# Probation Actions Workflow
This workflow defines the steps to manage employee probation, from initial KPI setting to final confirmation or extension.

// turbo-all
1. **Initial Assessment**: Review employee performance against initial expectations.
2. **KPI Setting**: Assign specific measurable goals for the probation period.
3. **Periodic Reviews**: Conduct 30, 60, and 90-day reviews to track progress.
4. **Final Decision**: Based on total performance, decide to Confirm, Extend, or Terminate.
5. **Letter Generation**: Generate and issue the relevant confirmation or extension letter.
6. **Notification**: Ensure all stakeholders (Employee, Manager, HR) are notified of the decision.
